What is socca.social

Socca Social is a social media agency that has one purpose and that is to scale social media marketing. Currently we are focussing solely on LinkedIn by matching businesses with LinkedIn assistants.

For who is it

Socca Social was created for businesses that heavily use LinkedIn but, having difficulties scaling there campaigns because of LinkedIn all the limitations that LinkedIn enforces. Some of the services that assistants can provide are messaging, increased engagement and prospecting.

How does it work

When you make a request for an assistant we will look into our database and try to match you with one of our available assistants. As soon as there is a match the assistant will optimize their profile for your campaign. All of our assistants are real people with real accounts so we are limited in optimizing profiles such as we will not add extra education or experiences, removal of education or experiences is possible. The optimization will happen in the description part.

What do you get

When a match is made you can add the assistant as an employee to your business on LinkedIn or the assistant can execute the campaign as a independent business developer or with any title required by the campaign.
